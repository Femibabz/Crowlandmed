Dr. Deji Makanjuola

DR Adedeji Adekunle Makanjuola is a dedicated family physician with a strong commitment to delivering comprehensive, patient-centred care. He is particularly keen on a holistic approach to healthcare, ensuring that his patients’ physical, emotional, and social well-being are all thoughtfully addressed.

He completed his postgraduate residency training in Family Medicine in the United Kingdom, where he gained extensive experience across a broad range of clinical settings.

Dr. Deji’s practice is guided by compassion, professionalism, and a commitment to empowering patients through education, prevention, and long-term health planning.
