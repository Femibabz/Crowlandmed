About Crowland Medical

Compassionate, Patient-Centered Healthcare

Conveniently located at 390 Crowland Ave, Welland, ON, our modern, fully equipped facility brings a wide range of medical services together in one accessible location.

Our experienced and caring team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing personalized care that supports your well-being at every stage of life. Whether you’re looking for a reliable family doctor, need immediate attention through our walk-in services, or want the convenience of our on-site pharmacy, we’re here to ensure your healthcare experience is smooth and reassuring.

  • Modern, fully equipped facility
  • Compassionate & personalized care
  • Trust, respect, and quality
