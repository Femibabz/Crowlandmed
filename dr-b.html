Dr. Akinpelu Babatunde (Dr. B.)

MBChB, MRCGP

Dr. Akinpelu Babatunde, MBChB, MRCGP, is a dynamic and experienced General Practitioner with a robust background in general and tropical medicine from Nigeria and complemented with a rigorous GP training and practice in the UK.

His practice emphasizes high-quality, patient-centered primary care. Dr. Babatunde is a forward-thinker, deeply invested in leveraging AI-assisted healthcare to enhance personalized patient outcomes.

As a respected leader, he serves as a Fellow with Health Education England and co-chair of the BMA East of England Forum for Racial and Ethnic Equality (FREE), mentoring and supporting international medical graduates and influencing healthcare policies.
